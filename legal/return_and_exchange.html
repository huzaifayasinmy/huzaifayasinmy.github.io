Return And Exchange

At Khaalis Store, we strive to provide our customers with high-quality Khalis Organic Products. However, we understand that sometimes products may not meet your expectations. Here’s how we handle returns and exchanges:



Eligibility for Return: Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they are unused, unopened, and in their original condition with the seal intact. Perishable items like food products or personal care items cannot be returned unless defective or damaged.



Defective or Damaged Products: If your product is defective, damaged, or incorrect, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving the item with a photo of the damage. We will arrange a free return and provide a replacement or refund based on availability.



Return Process: To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team . Our team will guide you through the return process and arrange for pickup.



Refunds: Refunds will be processed once the product is returned to us and inspected. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method within 7-10 business days.



Non-Returnable Items:

Opened or used items.

Perishable goods (e.g., organic food items).

Gift cards and vouchers.

Exchange Policy: If you prefer an exchange instead of a return, please contact our support team to check availability of the replacement item. Exchanges are subject to product availability.
