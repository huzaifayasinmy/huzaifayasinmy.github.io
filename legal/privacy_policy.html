Our Privacy Policy

At Khaalis Store, we take your privacy seriously. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. Below is an overview of how we collect, use, and safeguard your data:

Information We Collect: Personal Information: When you make a purchase, create an account, or contact us, we collect information such as your name, address, email, phone number, and payment details.

Order Information: We collect details about your purchases, including product names, quantities, and transaction history.

How We Use Your Information: To process orders, provide customer support, and communicate with you about your order status.

To personalize your shopping experience and provide recommendations.

For marketing purposes such as sending promotional offers, newsletters, and updates, but only with your consent. You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

Data Protection: We implement a variety of security measures to ensure that your personal information is secure. Your payment details are processed through a secure, encrypted gateway.



Third-Party Services:

We do not sell or trade your personal information to third parties. However, we may share necessary information with trusted third parties (e.g., delivery companies) to complete your order or provide services.



Cookies: Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and optimize functionality. You can adjust your browser settings to refuse cookies, though this may affect your ability to access certain features of our website.



Your Rights: You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. If you would like to make any changes or have concerns, please contact us at out contact email.



Changes to This Policy: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the effective date will be updated accordingly.
